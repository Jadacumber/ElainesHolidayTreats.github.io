Our History:

Elaine's Holiday Treats was created in October 2017 as a home business by Elaine Cumberland. Beforehand, she had been baking for her family and friends for years. We are a fully liscensed food service provider in the state of Virginia and our last inspection was in October of 2017.

Our Mission:

Our mission is to provide you, your family, and your friends with delicious homemade treats all year round. We have an annual tasting in the Fall to prepare for our active season. Information about this event will be released later on in the year.
